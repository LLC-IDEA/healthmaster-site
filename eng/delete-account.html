Account & Data Deletion

App: HealthMaster · Developer: TradeShield Network LLC

You can delete your HealthMaster account and personal data at any time. This page explains how to request deletion, what is deleted, what may be retained, and how long data is kept.

How to request deletion

  • In the app: open Settings → Account → Delete account and confirm. Your account and associated data are removed.
  • By email: write to support@healthmaster.app from the email address linked to your account, with the subject “Delete my data”. We process such requests within 30 days.

What data is deleted

  • Account and profile — email, name, user ID
  • Food and meal logs, calories and nutrition data
  • Weight, body measurements, and goals
  • Uploaded food photos and voice recordings
  • App-usage data tied to your account

What may be retained

  • Payment and transaction records that we are legally required to keep (for tax and accounting purposes), retained for the legally required period and stored separately from your profile.
  • Anonymized or aggregated data that can no longer identify you.

Retention period

Your personal data is deleted within 30 days of your request. Residual copies in encrypted backups are purged within 90 days.
