Delete Your HealthMaster Account

Last updated: June 2026

You can request deletion of your HealthMaster account and personal data at any time. This page explains exactly what gets deleted, how to request deletion, and how long it takes.

This action is permanent.

Once your account is deleted, all data listed below will be erased from our systems and cannot be restored. There is no undo and no recovery window — please make sure you really want to proceed before submitting a deletion request.

What gets deleted

When you delete your account, we erase the following data:

  • Profile information (name, age, sex, height, activity level, language)
  • Full meal history (photos, descriptions, calorie and macro logs)
  • Weight entries and weight history
  • Goals, target weight, daily calorie and macro targets
  • AI coach conversation history and personal recommendations
  • App settings, reminders, and notification preferences
  • Telegram user ID linkage and authentication tokens

How to delete your account

You have two ways to request account deletion:

Option 1 — In the mobile app

Open the HealthMaster app and go to:

Profile → Settings → Delete Account

Confirm the action when prompted. Your deletion request will be queued immediately and you will be signed out.

Option 2 — By email

Send an email to support@healthmaster.app with the subject line Delete Account.

In the body of the email, include the email address or Telegram username associated with your HealthMaster account so we can locate and verify it. We may reply to confirm the request before processing it.

How long it takes

Your data is removed from our active systems within 30 days of the request. Encrypted backups are rotated on a regular schedule and any residual copies are purged within 90 days of the original request.

Data we are required to keep

A small amount of data must be retained to comply with legal and accounting obligations even after account deletion:

  • Financial transaction records (invoices, payment receipts, refund records) are kept for 5 years as required by applicable tax and accounting law.
  • Anti-fraud and abuse logs may be retained in anonymized form for a limited period to protect the service and other users.

These records do not contain your meal history, weight, goals, or coaching conversations — only the minimum information required by law.

Questions or help

If you have any questions about the deletion process or need help locating your account, contact us at support@healthmaster.app.
