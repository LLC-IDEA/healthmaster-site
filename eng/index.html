Frequently asked

Do I need the app if I already use the bot?

No. The Telegram bot is a fully functional product on its own. The mobile app adds push reminders, offline mode, home-screen access and native charts — but every core feature works in Telegram too. Use whichever fits you better; data is synced.

Is there an iOS app?

Not yet. Android version is currently in Google Play internal testing and will be publicly available soon. iOS is on the roadmap. Meanwhile, Telegram bot works perfectly on iOS.

Which languages are supported?

Interface: English and Russian. Food recognition and the AI coach handle input in any language.

How is my data used?

We store your meal history, weight and measurements to give you personalized guidance. Payment data is processed by our payment provider — we never see card numbers. See our Privacy Policy.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. One tap in the app or a message to support. Refund policy is available here.
