Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

HealthMaster is a nutrition tracking and AI coaching service delivered via Telegram. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. Data We Collect

We may collect the following data:

  • Telegram user ID and basic profile information
  • Food logs and meal descriptions
  • Weight entries and goals
  • User messages sent to the bot

2. How We Use Data

  • To provide nutrition tracking and AI coaching
  • To generate personalized recommendations
  • To improve product functionality

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data with third parties. Payment processing is handled by third-party providers, such as YooKassa.

4. Data Storage

Data is stored securely and retained only as long as necessary to provide the service.

5. User Rights

Users may request deletion of their data at any time by contacting support.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data from unauthorized access or misuse.
